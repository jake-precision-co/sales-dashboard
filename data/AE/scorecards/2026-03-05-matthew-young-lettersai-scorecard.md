## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

→ **What he missed:** "The Golden Question" — stretching the dream with emotional depth before moving to roadblocks.

→ **What it is:** The Golden Question is the bridge from the logical dream ($2M ARR) to the personal/emotional dream. It sounds like: "That's amazing — $2M ARR. But I'm curious... what does that actually unlock for you? What changes in your life when you hit that number?" Then follow with: "How does that make you feel?" The goal is to get the prospect painting a vivid picture of their transformed life — not just their transformed spreadsheet. Per the playbook: "Don't fix — feel. Let the vision breathe."

→ **How to apply it on THIS call:** After Matt said "$2 million ready to go in recurring revenue" [22:03], Joe should have paused and said: "Love that, Matt. $2M ARR — that's a real business. But I'm curious... you mentioned you're running a moving company AND this SaaS AND you said you're 'running blind over here.' If we hit $2M and this thing is humming — what changes for you personally? Do you step back from the moving company? Do you hire a GM? What does your Tuesday morning look like?" Then let Matt talk for 2-3 minutes. THAT answer becomes the emotional fuel for the entire close: "Matt, you told me you want your Tuesdays back. You told me you're running blind. That's what this coaching is for — not just the revenue, but getting your life back."
