## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**Master the golden question.**

When a prospect asks "What do you guys do?" — that's not an invitation to pitch. That's an invitation to get curious.

**The script:**
"Great question — before I answer, help me understand: when you think about [their goal], what's challenging you more: [Option A] or [Option B]?"

Then PAUSE. Let them choose. Then ask: "Why does that one resonate with you most?"

That's the call. Everything else flows from there.

**Drill this:** Roleplay 10 cold calls this week where you ONLY practice asking the golden question and exploring the answer. Don't book anything. Just get good at curiosity.
