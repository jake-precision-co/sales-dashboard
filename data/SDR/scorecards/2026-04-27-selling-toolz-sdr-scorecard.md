## WHAT TO IMPROVE

- **Revenue was never confirmed.** Brian owns a large real estate brokerage (offline, Anti-ICP) and just acquired a SaaS company that's being revamped. JC never asked: what's the software company currently generating? Is it pre-revenue? Sub-$100K? This is the critical missing qual — if the SaaS company is pre-revenue and Brian's bandwidth is split between 120 agents and a dev team, this might not be the right fit right now.

- **The Calendly close is a ghost factory.** When Brian said "I don't have my calendar," JC had two options: (1) send Calendly link and hope, or (2) set a tentative time and let Brian adjust. Option 2 gets 3x more meetings. "No problem — how about Thursday at 10am ET as a placeholder? I'll send the invite and you can move it if needed." That converts the intent to a real commitment.

- **Ideal state and growth goals were never explored.** JC got current state (revamping, no sales motion, new to software sales). He never asked "What does growth look like for you — what do you want this software company to be doing in 12 months?" This is the KV current→ideal→gap framework, and the gap is what drives urgency. Without it, JC's recap was good but it landed on "this would help you build a foundation" — which is lower urgency than "you told me you want to go from $0 to $X in 12 months and here's what's blocking you."

- **Qualification on the business type needs depth.** Real estate CRM for 120-agent brokerages could be a niche vertical product — not obviously SaaS/AI/Vibe Coder ICP. JC should have asked: "Is this purely a recurring SaaS model? Who are the customers — other brokerages? Is this a software product with monthly subscriptions?" One more minute of qualification would have built a much stronger SQL case.

## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**Never send Calendly without a tentative time anchor first.**

The Calendly link is fine as a backup — but alone it's weak. Here's the exact language:

> "No problem at all. How about I put Thursday at 10am as a tentative placeholder — I'll send the invite now, and if that doesn't work you can reach out and we'll move it, or just use the link to reschedule. Does Thursday morning sound reasonable?"

This moves from "I hope they book" to "I have a tentative meeting unless they reschedule." Night and day on conversion.
