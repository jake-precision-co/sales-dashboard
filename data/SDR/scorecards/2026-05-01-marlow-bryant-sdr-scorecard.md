## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**Never let a call die on a brush-off. AAA every time.**

This call had an opening. Marlow engaged. He explained his reasoning. He said "maybe next year" — that's not a hard no. That's a timing objection with specific context attached (pre-AR, wants to figure it out first). JC had the information to ask a real follow-up question and didn't.

**Drill this:** Any time a prospect says "not now / not yet / maybe later," the ONLY acceptable response is an AAA. No exceptions.

> "Oh, totally fair — and I appreciate you being straight with me. Last thing I want is to push something that doesn't fit. Quick question before I let you go — when you say next year, what would need to change for this to make sense? Is it about getting to a revenue milestone, or is there something specific you're trying to figure out first?"

That question either surfaces urgency or gives you the real reason. Either way, you've learned something and you leave with intelligence.
