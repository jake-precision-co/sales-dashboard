## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

→ **Build the permission bridge before every discovery pivot.**

Right now JC jumps from "here's the email I'm resending" to "what are you trying to solve?" with no bridge. The prospect never said "yes, I'll talk." 

KV's exact formula: *"Hey — before I send that, do you mind if I ask you a quick question to make sure what I send is actually relevant to what you're working on?"* 

That single sentence accomplishes three things: (1) keeps the email as a carrot, (2) gets explicit permission, (3) makes the discovery feel helpful instead of intrusive. Drill this daily. Every callback needs this bridge.
