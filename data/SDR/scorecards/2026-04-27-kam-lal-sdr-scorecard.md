## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**→ Go deeper in discovery. You have one job when the prospect is talking: mine the vein, don't just collect the ore.**

Kam said: *"I've never been a good measure of things... I kind of find it hard to monitor and track things against experiments."*

JC: "So from what I'm hearing, you want something to show you what's working and what isn't."

**What KV/JM prescribes:**
> *"Tell me more about that — when you say you can't monitor experiments, what does that look like day-to-day? Like what happens when you want to know if something's working?"*
> *(Wait.)*
> *"And what's that costing you? Like if you can't figure out which channel is working, what does that mean for the business right now?"*

That's a KD curiosity chain + JM consequence question in 30 seconds. It takes the conversation from "he wants better tracking" (generic) to "he's making $10K/month after 5 years because he's been guessing and has no idea which experiments moved the needle" (specific, emotional, coachable). Joe can't close the first version. He can close the second.
