## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**Run the KV Email Redirect Play every time.**

When a prospect says "send me an email" or "text me info," that's NOT the end of the call — it's the setup for the golden question. Here's exactly what to say:

> "Absolutely — what's the best email for you? Great. Now I want to make sure what I send over is actually relevant. Real quick — typically when I talk to SaaS founders they're dealing with one of two things: either they're heads down trying to figure out how to get more consistent revenue, or they have decent revenue but they feel like they can't step back because nothing runs without them. Which one of those resonates with you most?"

That's the bridge. Use the email to open discovery, then use discovery to propose a specific time.
