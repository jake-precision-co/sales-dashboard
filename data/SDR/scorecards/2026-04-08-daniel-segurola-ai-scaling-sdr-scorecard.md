## TOP COACHING PRIORITY

**Re-engagement discovery on rebook calls.**

When a prospect picks up the phone on a rebook, they've already said yes once. That's a massive advantage. Use it. The moment they confirm who they are, ask: *"Perfect — just to make sure I'm still pointing you in the right direction, you mentioned wanting to build out the setter/closer model. Where's that at for you right now? What's the biggest gap you're trying to close?"*

Two questions. 60 seconds. Now JC has something to put in the recap. Now the invite has a reason attached to it. Now Joe walks into the Growth Session with context.

Without this, it's a logistics call. With it, it's a sales call.
