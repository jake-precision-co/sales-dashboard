# Prospect Roadblocks - Living Intelligence Doc

**Purpose:** Capture the exact words prospects use to describe what's blocking them from hitting their goals. This is competitive intelligence, objection library fuel, and messaging gold. Every roadblock here came from a real sales conversation.

**How to use:**
- **Sales prep:** Read 2-3 relevant entries before a call with a similar prospect profile
- **Messaging:** Use their language in ads, email sequences, and demo scripts
- **Product:** Surface recurring patterns to inform roadmap priorities
- **Coaching:** Show reps what great discovery sounds like - these are the quotes that unlock deals

**Tags explained:**
- **#industry** = Vertical or market (e.g., #saas #credit-unions #painting #direct-mail)
- **#mrr-range** = Revenue tier (e.g., #under-50k #50k-100k #100k-500k #500k-plus)
- **#closed-won / #closed-lost / #no-decision / #pipeline** = Deal outcome
- **#tier** = Precision tier discussed (#core-1k #growth-2k #scale-5k)
